Her Campus is an online magazine for female college students with indivual chapters from different universities around the world. I wrote a weekly article for the MSU chapter on topics like health, fitness, style and college life. I wanted to write articles that were relevant to girls in college and more specificially, girls at MSU. I used inspiration from events on campus, seasonal changes, student and female struggles to create pervasive content that would appeal to female students at MSU.
